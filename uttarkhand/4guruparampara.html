Genealogical Order of Lord Shiva

yataha pravruttirbhutanam yen sarvamidam tatam |
swakarmana tamabhyarchya siddhim vindati manvaha ||

GURUPARAMPARA

  1. Adinathanandnath adishaktyambika tatha
    Sadashivacha tachhaktim ishwarstasya shaktika.

  2. Rudrashchha rudrashaktyemba vishnustachhktireva cha
    Bramha cha bramhashaktyemba sanakachha sanandanam.

  3. Tatham sanatsujaatam syad rubhukshanand eva cha
    Tatham sanaatanaanandam syat sanatkumar eva cha.

  4. Dattatreyo raivatako vamadevstathaaparam
    vyasam shukoo nrusinhash mahesho bhaskaraabhidham.

  5. Mahendro madhavaanando vishnu devastatha param
    Gaudpaadaanandnatho govindanand eva cha.

  6. Tatham shreeshankarachryam padmapaadstathap param .
    Bimbatha vagbhavanandchhidanandam prasannakam.

  7. Sambrhamam paramatma cha nilakanthotha shambvam
    Vishwatmkam priyanandam krodisho veer eva cha.

  8. Chinmudram sauvichhaktyemba bijakashaktirev cha
    Bhattanandotha madano yogaanandstathottram.

  9. Bhimasinha shaakavruddhim sheelaabhattarikambika
    Bhattacha vikramaditya bhogam kllinnam kuleshwaram.

  10. Leelanandotha sahajam kamam kuleshwaram swaram
    Shreevidyanandnathcha dharamcharyeotho deepkam.

  11. Para bhattarikanandoghoram shaktidharomrutam
    Maatragupto muktakesham pratibha vishnudevakam.

  12. Vimalam sauvidanando bhojam parashivastatha
    Manojadevo gagano geyadityecha murtimaan.

  13. Adynashaktirmaurvikamba shivadasobhinandkam
    Dharakadambnaama cha bhuvananand eva cha.

  14. Maheshwarastrembkakho jayaguptam sadashivam
    Shwetambro devadaso durgasenotha parinim.

  15. Tatham kapilrudrakhyam shankuko deepakstatha
    Bhasam karnotpalanand upamannyurumapatim.

  16. Vidyapaticha harano vamanam sankulabhidham
    Tatasswad bhutapunyakhy uttpreksha vallbhabhidam.

  17. Ramanandam shivaswami kapolo rudratabhidham
    kumardasam shriharsham shambhum prathvishwaram smrutam.

  18. Hammiro vaidhynathachha kalidas strilechanam
    Ratnakaro lollatchha syanmatangadivakaram.

  19. Damodaram shrutatadharam sharmavarmaatha gobhilam
    Mayaraichaiva maghachha gopalo rajshekharam.

  20. Chanakyam krishnamishrichha kshemendro jayamadhavam.
    Ananto billahanoo dandi suravarmatha bhairavam.

  21. Trivikramo vararuchim krishnabhatto guheshwaram
    Saaraswato bhratharim kanthpandita eva cha.

  22. Chandraananoo naarmadevo bilwamangal eva cha
    Tata uttpalaraajaakhystatam shaktikumarkam.

  23. Arasi thakuranando bhavabhutishchha laxmanam
    Kridachandram shargnhadharo bharavirbhairavabhidham.

  24. Nagaamba kumudo banoo vaaklaranand eva cha
    Muktapidoo maadhurascha dharmakirtishcha hetukam.

  25. Nagavaidhyo bhojadevaha karpurashcha kutumbkam
    Nirvruttidevanaamach dnyanadevabhidhastatha.

  26. Muktamba bhallatanando bhattaswami tathaha param
    Laxmidharo harigano vidyateerthabhidhastadha.

  27. Vidyaranyaha sayanaha syadindurajashchyaha rasakaha
    Govindarajo bhanuscha jayadeo maheshwaraha.

  28. Peeyushavarsho deveshaha sarvadnyaha shrungabhoopatihi
    Vishnugupto yogabhatto bheribhankar eva cha

  29. Tato raghava chaitanyastataha shankadhar smrutaha
    bhattarako vishnusharma tato govardhanodharaha.

  30. Dhanapalo murarischa subandhuhrradattakaha
    Pushpakaraschotkatscha tat anandadevakaha.

  31. Vimarshashchhaiva purnashcha rajadevoo vinayakam
    Tatoo vallabhadevaakhyam Swabhavaanand eva cha.

  32. Hariharadevanama cha dharmnarayanabhidham
    Subhagoo vai parimaloo revadasam prashaktakam.

  33. Vasundharotha kumudanandoo govindbhattakam
    Anandshcha prakashashcha bhasuranand eva cha.

    SWAYAM PRAKASHANANDANATHAHA
    AMRUTTESHANANDANATHAHA ( Martand Dikshit)
    POORNANANDANATHAHA (Shri Chidambar Mahaswaminaha).
