Bajirao Peshwe

Adnyanatohi yadnyatam papam tasyapratikriya |
kathita dharma shastradnyaihi sakamasya na vidyate ||

After the historic second yadnya (Somayaga ) in Kaliyuga (the first one is done in the dwaparayuga by Shri Yudhistara with the help of lord Shri Krishna ). Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji lived at Navalgund on the request of his disciples and villagers. The Somayaga greateness reached to every corner of the country. The biggest king of the time the Bajirao Peshwae second of Pune has arrived to Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji for darshana along with his ministry and huge military. Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji gave all the respect and suitable treatment, hospitality particularly many types of food items to the king his people .

The fallowers of king Pashave and some ministers surprised. And praised Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji about the arrangemets and treatment. The king Bajirao Pashave himself surprised with all this. And asked Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji how it can be made possible. Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji told that it is all with the help of people like you, nothing is mine. The people of Navagund also helped to serve you and your huge military so please take this mantrakshata and sriphala so as to continue you as per your next day plan .

The king Bajirao Pashave wanted to test Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji or even he wanted to defame Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji in his mind. So he requested Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji to allow him to stay one more day and enjoy all the things like yesterday. Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji told, I am a simple Brahamin who is living on the help of others like you, why you are testing me. Even after you know better it is much difficult to manage the huge military. But the king has not agreed and stayed forcefully for the second day. Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji have no choice other than to supply food shelter etc, as they were guests of him. On the second day all the arrangements like as per earlier day were made with more fine. The food items also prepaed very much tasty or test fully made and supplied to entire military along with ministers and kings with Bajirao. The king Bajirao surprised and he prostrated in front of Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji with a shameful heart. In total around 85500 people took meals.

Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji told him "Oh king listen carefully that I am giving you mantrakshatas and sriphala which are more holly and more precious for you as well as for the country men, after accepting them you only should take them upto your palace or home. Then Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji closed his eyes with whispering some Mantras gave Sri Phala and Mantrkashata to Bajirao Pashave. The king accepted but handed over them to the servant near by standing to Peshwa. To see the situation the eyes of Shri Chidambar Mahaswamiji become wet, tears dropped down. These were the drops of patriotism which the king understood lateron

Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji told "Oh king you have handed over your everything to the servant, its not only your bad luck but also of the nation. You have not taken the caution.The king didn’t understood the meaning and returned to Sondur. Shri Chidambara Mahaswamiji told that the days to come are very difficult abrahamnya days are waiting.Later on the British rule came into India in a fulsome.

During the time at Navalgund the marriage of Shri Mrutyunjaya with Kum Mhalasa the daughter of Shri Bapu businessman at Pune. And Wararuchi 's marriage with Kum Rama, daughter of Mayaral (Mairal) Naik from Kolhapur was also over .
